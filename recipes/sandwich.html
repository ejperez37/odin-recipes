Sandwich

Description

In the following paragraphs, we will be going over a recipe to cook the perfect grilled cheese sandwich!

The perfect balance of cheese and bread will be achieved, and you will be able to bring out the best of your chef skills. Get your skillets heated and ovens turned on, let's get into it!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Throw cheese in between bread.
  2. Spread butter on both sides.
  3. Cook combination for 2 minutes per side.
  4. Enjoy!

Steak

Description

In the following paragraphs, we will be going over a recipe to cook the perfect medium-rare steak!(You can adjust the cook of the steak as needed if you enjoy eating steak the wrong way ;))

The perfect balance of herbs and lemon will be achieved, and you will be able to bring out the best of the cut of steak that you have chosen. Get your skillets heated and ovens turned on, let's get into it!

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Lemons
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet to 300000 degrees farenheit.
  2. Cook steak for 3 minutes per side.
  3. Put steak in skillet into oven for .5 seconds.

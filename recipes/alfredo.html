Description

In the following paragraphs, we will be going over a recipe to cook the perfect alfredo dish!

The perfect balance of alfredo and pasta. Get your pans heated and aprons on, let's get into it!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix ingredients into a bowl.
  4. Combine mixture and pasta on low heat.
